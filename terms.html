1. What Scheme Staff is (and isn't)

Scheme Staff is a matching platform: we connect property management companies with candidates in the community scheme industry. We are not a party to any employment relationship. Any employment contract, engagement or payment arrangement concluded between an employer and a candidate is strictly between those two parties.

2. Registration and accuracy

By registering, you confirm that the information you provide is true, accurate and up to date, and that you are authorised to provide it (for companies: that you may act on the company's behalf). Misrepresenting qualifications, experience or company details may result in removal from the platform.

3. Matching

We match candidates and roles based on the information both parties provide. A match is an introduction, not a guarantee: we do not guarantee that any candidate will be hired or that any role will be filled. Candidates may decline a match. Employers are responsible for their own hiring decisions, including interviews, reference checks and employment terms.

4. Verification

Where a profile or qualification is marked "verified", it means Scheme Staff has taken reasonable steps to confirm it with the issuing institution or relevant body. Verification is offered in good faith and does not replace an employer's own due diligence.

5. Fees

Fees for employer services are as published on our fees page or agreed in writing. Registering and being matched is free for candidates. Fees may change from time to time; changes will not affect services already paid for.

6. Acceptable use

  • Do not misuse information received through the platform (for example, contact details of matched parties) for any other purpose.
  • Do not post content that is unlawful, defamatory or misleading.

7. Privacy
