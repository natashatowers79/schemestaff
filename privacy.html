Privacy Policy

How Scheme Staff collects, uses and protects your personal information, in line with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

Last updated: July 2026

1. Who we are

Scheme Staff ("we", "us") operates schemestaff.co.za, a recruitment platform connecting property management companies with candidates in the community scheme industry in South Africa. For any privacy-related query, contact us via our contact page.

2. What we collect

When you register or use the platform, we collect the information you provide, which may include:

  • Candidates: name, ID or passport number, date of birth, contact details, address, education, employment history, qualifications and certificates, PPRA registration, salary information, availability and work preferences, and documents you upload.
  • Employers: company details, CIPC registration number, VAT number, contact person details, portfolio information, compliance documents, and job posting details.
  • Everyone: messages you send us through the site.

3. Why we collect it

We use this information to operate the platform: creating your profile, matching candidates with roles, verifying qualifications where requested, communicating with you, and administering our services. We will not use your information for unrelated purposes without your consent.

4. Who we share it with

  • Matching: relevant parts of a candidate's profile are shared with employers they are matched with (and vice versa) — that is the purpose of the platform. Sensitive details such as salary information are only shared as described on the relevant forms.
  • Verification: where you ask us to verify a qualification, we may contact the issuing institution.
  • Service providers: we use reputable third-party services (such as Google) to store and process data on our behalf.
  • We do not sell your personal information to anyone.

5. How we protect it

Your information is stored on access-controlled systems, and we limit access to those who need it to operate the platform. Passwords are never stored in our registration records.

6. How long we keep it

We keep your information for as long as your profile is active, or as needed to comply with legal obligations. You may ask us to delete your profile and associated data at any time.

7. Your rights

Under POPIA you have the right to access the personal information we hold about you, ask us to correct or delete it, object to processing, and lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator (South Africa) — inforegulator.org.za.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page, with the date above updated accordingly.
