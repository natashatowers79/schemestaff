About us

Built by the industry,

Scheme Staff is a specialist recruitment platform for South Africa's community scheme and property management sector.

Why we exist

Property management hiring is specialised. Job boards aren't.

Finding a property manager who understands trust accounts, a caretaker who can handle a 400-unit scheme, or a bookkeeper who knows levy administration takes more than a generic job advert. General job boards bury the right people under hundreds of irrelevant CVs — and the industry's best candidates often aren't looking on them at all.

Scheme Staff was created from inside the sectional title and HOA management world to solve exactly this: connecting managing agents with staff who already speak the language of the industry.
